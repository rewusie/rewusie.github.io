About Me

My name is Rebeka Ewusie, and I am currently a junior studying journalism at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland, College Park. I am interested in copy editing, radio, and broadcast journalism. I have articles published with Her Campus, Stories Beneath the Shell and Mitzpeh, and I was an anchor at WMUC News in 2019.
